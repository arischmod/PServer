template: ster?clnt=<name|pass>&com=fndusrs&str=str_name
          Order of query params is not important. Stereotype name should
	  not end with *.

	  but match it's rule. The initial degree is 1 for those users.
example : ster?clnt=name|pass&com=fndusrs&str=testStr1
